Canberra Ruby Crew February meeting wrap-up

2008-02-23

The second Canberra Ruby Crew meeting was a success. Our number grew to seven, and all had a good time chatting, eating, playing guitar hero and sharing ideas and code. Thanks to everyone who came along.

Some things we did:

  • Hugh introduced some of the crew to the wonders of Haml and Sass and bringing them together with StaticMatic
  • Shoaib ran us through how to get started with git and gitorious
  • Matt showed off his use of the Stencil plugin and his rocking Open Flash Charts.

The next meeting will be on Friday the 21st of March. Keep your eyes on the Rails Oceania google group for an announcement closer to the date!

Edit: The next meeting will actualy be on Friday 28th March, beginning a last-Friday-of-the-month pattern.
